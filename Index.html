ABCDE Melanoma Detection Method
A
Asymmetry
One half doesn't match the other in shape
B
Border
Ragged, notched, blurred, or irregular edges
C
Color
Multiple shades: brown, black, red, white, blue
D
Diameter
Larger than 6mm (pencil eraser); melanomas can be smaller
E
Evolving
Any change in size, shape, color, or new symptoms like bleeding
⚕ Visual AI analysis is for educational awareness only and cannot diagnose cancer. A qualified dermatologist must evaluate any concerning lesion. If you notice ABCDE warning signs, consult a physician promptly.
